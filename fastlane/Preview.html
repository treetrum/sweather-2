Description
Sweather 2 - a beautifully simple weather app for iOS.

Now completely rebuilt for version 2!

Sweather 2 shows you weather details for your current location and up to 5 saved locations using a beautiful design.

Featured include:
• Actual & Apparent Temperatures
• Rainfall Probability
• Localised Rain Radar
• Weather Summary with over 30 accompanying custom icons
• 24 hour temperature forecast
• 7 day forecast with detailed summary
• 21 custom icons to choose from
• Up to 5 saved locations

Weather data provided by Willy Weather.
